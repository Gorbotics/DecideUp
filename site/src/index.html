Get Started

group

Group up

Easily form groups with people you want to make decisions with. This could be friends and family, political organizations or church groups. Create a group and start inviting!

chat

Debate

A member posts a topic to vote on. The system then manages the debate allowing group members to respond to the strongest arguments in real time. The strongest arguments will float to the top of the dicussion allowing for more clear understanding of the issue.

check

Vote

Members can vote at any time and change their vote at any time during the poll period. Anonymous voting statistics will be shown in real time during the debate. After the final roll call, all votes are final.
